About Us

  • 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    My Louisville based venture began during the summer of 1985. I was waiting to catch a military hop out of Travis AFB (now closed) and had to suffer through a 3-day delay. With Napa valley being within a short drive, I decided to go there and wait out my delay.

    That's where the discovery began; I was hooked on the beauty, the landscape, the wines, the foods and the incredibly friendly people of Napa. Ever since, I’ve been going back for my yearly “pilgrimage” once or twice a year for over 30 years. Over the years, my knowledge of their wines, wineries and winemakers has expanded and deepened. I hope to share this knowledge with you in three ways:
    (1) wine tasting experience,
    (2) wine and food pairing dinners and
    (3) a guided tour of the best wine producing region in the world.
